About Us & Programs

Discover our mission, leadership, and comprehensive training programs

Who We Are

Our Mission

To empower fashion designers in Ghana through comprehensive education, professional development, and industry networking, while promoting sustainable and culturally-rich fashion practices.

Our Vision

To establish Ghana as a leading fashion hub in Africa, recognized globally for innovative designs that blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics.

Core Values

  • Excellence in craftsmanship and design
  • Cultural preservation and innovation
  • Sustainable and ethical fashion practices

Our Journey & Achievements

2015

Foundation

FDAG was established by a group of passionate fashion designers committed to elevating Ghana's fashion industry.

2017

First Training Program

Launched our flagship Professional Fashion Design Course with 25 pioneering students.

2019

International Recognition

FDAG received UNESCO recognition for promoting cultural heritage through fashion education.

2021

Sustainable Initiative

Launched our Sustainable Fashion Initiative, training over 200 designers in eco-friendly practices.

2024

1000+ Graduates

Celebrated training over 1,000 fashion professionals who now contribute significantly to Ghana's economy.

Our Programs

Professional Fashion Design Course

6 Months GHS 2,500

Comprehensive program covering design fundamentals, pattern making, garment construction, and fashion illustration.

  • Design theory and color principles
  • Pattern making and draping
  • Sewing and construction techniques
  • Fashion illustration and CAD

Fashion Business Masterclass

3 Months GHS 1,800

Learn the business side of fashion including marketing, branding, pricing, and building your fashion empire.

  • Business planning and strategy
  • Brand development and marketing
  • Financial management and pricing
  • E-commerce and digital marketing

Sustainable Fashion Initiative

2 Months GHS 1,200

Discover eco-friendly design practices, sustainable materials, and ethical production methods.

  • Sustainable material sourcing
  • Zero-waste design techniques
  • Ethical production practices
  • Circular fashion principles
