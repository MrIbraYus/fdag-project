About FDAG

The Fashion Designers Association of Ghana (FDAG) is the premier organization dedicated to advancing the art and business of fashion design in Ghana. We provide comprehensive training, networking opportunities, and professional development for aspiring and established fashion designers.

Our mission is to elevate Ghanaian fashion on the global stage while preserving our rich cultural heritage through innovative design and sustainable practices.
